Age of War

About Age of War

The legendary real-time strategy game that defined the Flash era! Start in the Stone Age and evolve your army through the ages — from cavemen and archers to knights, musketeers, robots, and futuristic units. Build your base, generate gold, train soldiers, upgrade tech, and destroy the enemy's base before they destroy yours. With simple yet addictive gameplay, satisfying progression, and epic boss battles, Age of War is one of the most replayed and influential tower defense / RTS hybrids ever made!

Original Creator: Louissi (Louie Cordero)
First Released: 2007
Current Version on site: Classic Flash version (via Ruffle emulator)
Added to Unblocked Game Zone: January 2026

Controls:
• Mouse → Click buttons to buy units, upgrade, and change era
• 1–9 Keys → Quick-select unit types (optional)
• Spacebar → Pause / Speed up game (in some versions)

Pro Tip: Rush early with cheap units to disrupt the enemy economy — then tech up fast to overwhelm them with stronger forces!
