About Alien Hominid

The legendary Flash prototype that started it all! Crash-land on Earth, get chased by the FBI, and blast your way through waves of agents in this fast-paced, one-hit-death run 'n' gun shooter. Inspired by classics like Metal Slug and Contra, you play as a tiny yellow alien with a massive blaster — shoot, jump, throw grenades, and bite heads off enemies in hilarious style. Short, brutal, and insanely addictive — this is the original Newgrounds hit that led to the full console versions!

Original Creators: Tom Fulp (programming) & Dan Paladin (art)
First Released (Flash prototype): August 7, 2002
Current Version on site: Original Newgrounds Flash prototype (via Ruffle emulator)
Added to Unblocked Game Zone: January 2026

Controls:
• Arrow Keys → Move & aim (Up = aim up, Down = duck/crawl)
• A / Z → Shoot blaster
• S / X → Jump
• Down + Jump (in air) → Bite/head-stomp enemy
• Space / Shift → Throw grenade (limited)

Pro Tip: One hit = death — stay mobile, use grenades on groups, and don't get greedy!
