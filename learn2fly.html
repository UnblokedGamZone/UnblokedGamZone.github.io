About Learn to Fly 2

Revenge is a dish best served airborne! In Learn to Fly 2 (2011 sequel by Light Bringer Games), the determined penguin is back — but icebergs crushed your dreams last time. Launch your upgraded contraption, destroy obstacles, smash snowmen for extra cash, and buy even better sleighs, gliders, boosts, payloads, and secret "omega" items to fly farther and finally conquer the Antarctic. New modes: Story (campaign with plot & revenge), Classic (endless distance), Arcade (challenge runs). More upgrades, better physics, and tons of addictive progression — the penguin will fly... or die trying!

Original Creator / Developer: Light Bringer Games (lightbringer777)
First Released: June 16, 2011 (on Kongregate, Armor Games, Newgrounds)
Current Version on site: Classic Flash version (via Ruffle emulator)
Added to Unblocked Game Zone: January 2026

Controls:
• Left Arrow / A → Tilt nose up (gain height, lose speed)
• Right Arrow / D → Tilt nose down (gain speed, lose height)
• Spacebar → Activate boost / rockets / special abilities (once unlocked)
• Any key (or customizable) → Trigger special sleighs/payloads
• Mouse → Shop menus, customize controls in options

Pro Tip: Prioritize sleigh speed & glider stability early — then boost & payload for smashing icebergs. In Story mode, focus on destroying obstacles for progress; in Arcade, chain crashes for massive cash. Balance angle: too steep = short flight, too flat = crash fast. Unlock omega items for game-breaking power!
