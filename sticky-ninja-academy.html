About Sticky Ninja Academy

Enroll in the ultimate ninja training and become a Sticky Ninja Master! Sticky Ninja Academy is the classic 2011 Flash hit by LongAnimals. Use precise mouse drags to launch your ninja like a slingshot, stick to surfaces, bounce off springs, defeat enemy ninjas with deadly accuracy, avoid deadly spikes & traps, and reach the exit door in each of the 30 challenging levels. Aim for perfection: fewer jumps = higher scores! A perfect blend of physics-based puzzle action and ninja flair.

Original Creator / Developer: LongAnimals (programming), biscuitlocker (art)
First Released: 2011 (November on Newgrounds & Armor Games)
Current Version on site: Classic Flash version (via Ruffle emulator)
Added to Unblocked Game Zone: January 2026

Controls:
• Mouse → Drag and release to launch/jump the ninja (like a slingshot)
• Click & drag on empty space → Look around / plan your shot
• Stick to most black/wooden surfaces → Use them to climb or redirect
• Bounce off springy objects → Gain height or speed
• Avoid spikes & slick surfaces that make you slide!

Pro Tip: Kill all enemies to open the exit — fewer jumps means a better score! Experiment with bounces and sticky walls to chain perfect runs. Smash barrels for hidden surprises!
